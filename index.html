Front End Product

Cocktail Drink Library

Spirit Guide

Tasked with consuming an API of various cocktail drink recipes, Spirit Guide's aim was to make a responsive and user friendly interface to display everyone's favorite drink for any occasion.

Spirit Guide was built using a fairly basic front end stack of HTML, CSS, and Javascript, but it's intuitive and flexible design allows users to sift through hundreds of drink options easily by name or spirit type. When the user first opens the application, they are presented with a variety of popular drink recipes immediately at their fingertips, but what delicious and easy to understand recipe they decide to create, is entirely up to them.

  • Project Type: Front End Application
  • Duration: 1 Week
  • Stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Full Stack Project

Healthy Me! A Wellness App

Healthy Me

Achievements worth striving for rarely come without careful planning and thinking ahead towards the future.

Healthy Me was created with that very sentiment in mind from the ground up. Allowing users to register and login to their own personal accounts, Healthy Me gives the user the ability to build physical, mental, and long term goals with the functionality to add notes and comments to each individual goal. In addition to setting individual goals, the app gives users the ability to upload their own personal profile picture giving the app a sense of ownership and personality.

  • Project Type: Full Stack Application
  • Duration: 1 Week
  • Stack: PostgreSQL, NodeJS, Sequelize, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
Design

An HTML Redesign for the Modern Market

High On Coding Redesign

While websites from the early days of the internet are important artifacts of history and fun to look at, they don't make for the best responsive designs in the modern era.

Tasked with transforming a clunky and forgotten website from the early 2000s into a responsive site that would function on modern devices, HoC is a proof of concept that just because something is older, doesn't mean it can't be adapted to fit in with the here and now (see my professional history). The overhauling of High on Coding's front end was done so with the mobile experience in mind from the ground up making this application useable on any device.
